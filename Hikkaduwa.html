Introduction

Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a renowned conservation project dedicated to protecting and preserving sea turtles. Located in the beautiful coastal town of Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka, the hatchery plays a vital role in ensuring the survival of these magnificent creatures.

Services

  • Rescue and rehabilitation of injured or sick turtles
  • Educational tours and interactive sessions
  • Turtle nest protection and hatching programs
  • Release of rehabilitated turtles into the sea
