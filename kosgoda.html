Introduction

Welcome to Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, a conservation project aimed at protecting and preserving sea turtles. Our hatchery is located on the beautiful coast of Sri Lanka, and we are dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of sea turtle conservation.

At our hatchery, we collect sea turtle eggs, protect them, and release the baby turtles back into the ocean once they hatch. We also provide a safe haven for injured turtles, nursing them back to health before releasing them.
