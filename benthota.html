Introduction

Welcome to Benthota Turtle Hatchery, a conservation project dedicated to protecting and preserving sea turtles. Located in the beautiful coastal town of Benthota, Sri Lanka, our hatchery aims to raise awareness about the importance of sea turtle conservation and ensure the survival of these magnificent creatures.

At our hatchery, we collect sea turtle eggs, provide a safe environment for their incubation, and release the hatchlings into the ocean once they are strong enough to survive. We also care for injured turtles, nurse them back to health, and release them back into their natural habitat.
