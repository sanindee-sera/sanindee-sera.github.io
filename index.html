OUR GOAL

To protect and conserve sea turtle populations through responsible breeding, hatching, and release programs. We are committed to ensuring the survival and well-being of these magnificent creatures by providing a safe and nurturing environment for their eggs and hatchlings. Through education and outreach, we strive to raise awareness about the importance of sea turtle conservation and inspire individuals to take action in preserving their habitats. Our ultimate goal is to contribute to the recovery of sea turtle populations and create a sustainable future for these endangered species.

Effort

Currently, the hatchery and rescue center focuses on the well-being of five turtle species. These egg mounds are carefully monitored to track the hatching progress of the turtle eggs

Types of turtles

  • The Olive Ridley Turtle,
  • Leatherback Turtle,
  • Loggerhead Turtle,
  • Hawksbill Turtle,
  • Green Turtle

Our Duty

Rehabilitating injured sea turtles and safeguarding their nesting grounds. Its primary objective is to protect and preserve the sea turtles that grace the golden shores of the island.
